Television

Make an impact and make it fast.

In the age of nano-second attention spans, grabbing the viewer's attention in the first few seconds is critical. Getting the consumer's attention is only the beginning.

Check Out Our TV Magic

Read more about television advertising

Television remains the most influential and one of the most cost-efficient mediums by which to deliver your message to consumers. Yet, getting a potential customer’s attention is only the beginning. Delivering a memorable message to your desired demographic while, maintaining and reinforcing your brand's image takes the kind of skill only 30-plus years of experience and thousands of produced commercials can provide.

With the television experience evolving beyond the traditional living room to anytime, anywhere, and anyhow, it takes keen planning and a thorough buying process to navigate through this expanding media landscape. Using our state-of-the-art in-house editing suite, Miller Ad Agency creates engaging ads with your specific objectives in mind, within in a matter of days. We strategically place those ads on the programs that offer your message the maximum amount of exposure to bring you a bigger ROI.

Radio

Get in-tune with your audience.

Radio is going through a renaissance.

Currently, with 91% of consumers listening to AM/FM radio at least weekly, traditional radio provides the opportunity to target specific types of listeners on a local wide basis; while digital streaming services such as Pandora can offer the opportunity to target as granular as zip codes.

Whether you need to reach people on a national level or need more of a local connection, Radio offers the opportunity to scale the delivery of your message as big or as small as needed.

Email Marketing

Get in your customer's inbox.

Email Marketing can be a measurable, cost-efficient way to update your consumer base on your business with powerful results. So, you’ll know exactly what’s calling out to them.

View eBlast examples

Read more about email marketing

Email marketing is a broad tool with the purpose that can:

  • Initiate a relationship of trust between you and your customers.
  • Create brand awareness for your product or service.
  • Encourage customer loyalty and repeat business.

Email marketing has become, over the years, increasingly more popular due to:

  • How often we check or send email every day. I'm sure you just did 5 minutes ago.
  • You can track an exact return on your investment.
  • It is significantly faster and cheaper than traditional mail.
  • You can reach email subscribers who have already agreed to receive information on subjects that interest them. a.k.a. potentially your products/services.
  • Email marketing is only second to search marketing as the most effective digital marketing tactic. Again, when did you last check your email?
